My Exciting Life as a Software Engineer

Life has never been the same since my first hello world. I take you deeper into my story into the world of software development/engineering or whatever you'd like to choose to call me. At the end of the day I do this one thing, the thing all software devs do, I solve problems. Then, I use programming languages to execute those solutions. I can also make cool stuff change colors and bounce up and down on the screen. Yeah, It's a bit like having some sort of super power. If you're also a software engineer reading this then you know what I'm talking about. With just your keyboard, your mouse and your brain you can make entire applications using code. I hope you enjoy my blog and don't be afraid to reach out. Be you, unapologetically and unafraid. This is, a developer's life. - Luis Aguilera Garcia
